Get the most out of Grovy

Quick start tips

Tip 1
Scan your receipt first
After a grocery run, use the AI receipt scanner to add everything at once. It's way faster than manual entry.
Tip 2
Type produce names
For fruits and veggies, just type the name — Grovy will auto-suggest a shelf life and storage tip.
Tip 3
Keep notifications on
Lemmy's alerts are the whole point. They're funny, I promise. You'll actually want to read them.
Tip 4
Check Stats weekly
The Stats tab shows how much money you've saved. It's genuinely satisfying to watch that number grow.
